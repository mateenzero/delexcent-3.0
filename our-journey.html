  • 2009

    Delexcent Systems incorporated
    The foundation of Delexcent was laid in 2009 by three friends, Mohammed Abdul Mateen, Sanoj Salam, and Arif Roomy in Bangalore, India. With a shared passion for technology, we embarked on a journey to deliver innovative digital and IT solutions.
  • 2011
    Delexcent’s Golden Age
    Five years into our journey, Delexcent expanded its reach by securing clients from the US, Canada, and the Middle East, including government projects. We grew alongside our success, moving to a larger office to support our expanding operations.
  • 2019
    Delexcent’s Turning Point
    By 2019, Delexcent expanded further, establishing branch offices in Hyderabad and Cochin, strengthening our presence and capabilities to serve a broader client base.
  • 2024
    Strategic Expansion
    Delexcent expanded into the Middle East, focusing on Cloud & DevOps, Cybersecurity, and Staffing Services to drive digital transformation across the region.
Delexcent Systems delivers cutting-edge solutions in Digital Transformation, Cloud, Cybersecurity, and Managed Services, empowering businesses with scalable, secure, and automation-driven technologies for seamless growth.
